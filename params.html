Aclaraciones
  • Strength: Bodily might in terms of power.
  • Endurance: How much damage one can withstand.
  • Agility: Quickness and speed of reaction.
  • Magical Power: How much magical energy can be handled.
  • Good luck: The quality of one's luck.
  • Noble Phantasm: The strength of the Noble Phantasm one owns.
  • A status showing a clear representation of the mentality of the Servant. It represents a combination of the policy they place importance on and their personality
  • Personalities: good, neutral, evil.
  • Policies: lawful, neutral, chaotic.
  • Non-matching personalities won't result in major discord between servants
  • Differing policies will result in difficulty in a Servant resolving differences of opinion with another Servant.
